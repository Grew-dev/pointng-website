Why use pointNG in location personalization?

Hyper Secure: coordinate data never leaves the client

We have created a unique way to apply neural networks to do reverse geocoding straight in the client interface. This technology never sends coordinate data over the internet to 3rd party services.

→ How it works in detail

Easy To Use Functions

You don't have to worry about complex setup or endless documentation browsing. We have paid special attention to make pointNG as easy to use as possible. Just plug it in and get started immediately.

→ Check out the documentation and get started

Join the Future: empower the user

pointNG introduces a human-centric, non-creepy approach to developing location-aware digital services. Our widget gives the user the power to determine the level of accuracy in location identification.
